The Tasting

What you taste, slowly.

I, Aroma

"Wild flowers, warm hay."

As the water is poured a delicate floral steam rises, dried mountain blossom, warm hay and a faint breath of citrus from the high alpine meadows.

II, Cup

"Golden, light, soothing."

The infusion settles to a clear golden yellow. Smooth and naturally sweet, with gentle herbal notes and a softness that needs no sugar to be complete.

III, Finish

"Clean, calm, lingering."

A quiet, clean finish that lingers like the mountain air it came from, caffeine free and naturally calming, made for slow evenings.

Wild mountains · 1100m · Summer harvest
The Origin

Wild on the mountain.

Greek mountain tea grows wild, never farmed, high on the sunlit slopes above 1100 metres, among rocky ground and clean alpine air. It is gathered by hand each summer, the way it has been for generations.

The stems are dried slowly and whole, flowers intact, then sealed to keep the aroma alive. A caffeine-free infusion, treasured across the Mediterranean as part of the daily wellness ritual.
