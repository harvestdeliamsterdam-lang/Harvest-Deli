I.Why partner with the house

A quiet, premium programme.

Four assurances we make to every partner. Nothing performative. Each one reflects how the cellar actually works.

I.

Premium quality

Single estate, cold extracted, never blended. Each jar carries an edition number and a season. The standard never moves.

II.

Fast European shipping

Pallet and parcel logistics across the EU. Tracked, insured, signed delivery. Two to five working days from the cellar.

III.

Hospitality ready

Trade pricing tiers, branded tasting boxes, menu cards and pairing notes prepared for chefs, sommeliers and concierge teams.

IV.

Private label possibilities

For hotels, brands and concept stores. Custom embossing, label design and dedicated allocations from a chosen estate.
