Our story

A house on a single mountain.

Harvest Deli began on the southern face of Pelion, where one family has kept bees for five generations. What leaves the mountain is small by nature, a few hundred jars a season, gathered slowly and sealed by hand.

We work with olive groves and wild tea hillsides in the same spirit: little, seasonal and honest. (Placeholder copy, the full story will follow.)
